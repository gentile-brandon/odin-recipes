Asparagus Ricotta Flatbread

Description

A hearty, delicious flatbread, perfect for a summer afternoon.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices naan
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 medium leeks, thinly sliced crosswise
  • 1 bundle asparagus cut into 1/2 in pieces
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
  • 4 tablespoons Lemon Vinaigrette
  • 1/3 cup Ricotta cheese
  • 4 teaspoons Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Baste the naan with 1 tablespoon of olive oil and sprinkle salt and pepper. Place on a rimmed baking sheet and lightly toast for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. While the naan is toasting, heat the remaining olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Add the leeks and asparagus and saute until softened, 8 to 10 minutes. Stir in the thyme and season with salt and pepper. Transfer into medium bowl and toss with 3 tablespoons of the lemon Vinaigrette.
  4. Spread ricotta across the naan and top with the asparagus and leeks.
  5. Serve with a final drizzle of the Vinaigrette and a few sprinkles of freshly grated parmesan cheese.
