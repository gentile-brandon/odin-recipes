TikTok Feta Pasta

Description

This tasty pasta utilizes rustic ingredients and fresh basil for a quick and easy home-cooked meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb your choice of pasta (we use farfalle)
  • 3 shallots, diced
  • 1 lb cherry tomatoes
  • I large block feta cheese
  • Handful fresh basil, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. In a 9x13 baking dish, add cherry tomatoes, shallots, garlic, and olive oil. Mix to coat.
  3. Place your block of feta in the center of the baking dish, making sure it is flat against the bottom. Bake for 30 minutes.
  4. Place a large pot of water on the stove and bring it to boil. Cook your pasta according to instructions on the package.
  5. Once done, strain your pasta. Remember to set aside a little bit of pasta water.
  6. Take your baking dish out of the oven. Make sure all the cherry tomatoes have burt open. If not, just poke them with a spoon. Mix to combine.
  7. Add in your pasta, and a little pasta water the thicken the sauce. Mix to combine.
  8. Immediately stir in the fresh basil and serve!
